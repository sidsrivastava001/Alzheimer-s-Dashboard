About

Our mission is to provide Alzehimer's patients with the opportunity to connect with doctors from a remote location. Patients can submit their data through our mobile app for the doctor to analyze and then diagnose the patient. Doctors can keep track of their patients by setting up appointments and viewing their data through our website.
